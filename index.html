Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I save with solar?+
Most homes reduce their electricity bill by 90–100%, depending on system size and roof sun exposure.
How does government subsidy work?+
Residential rooftop systems are eligible for central government subsidy under current schemes (1kW = ₹30,000, 2kW = ₹60,000 and 3kW & above = ₹78,000). We handle the full application for you.
How long does installation take?+
Typically 7–10 days from site survey to commissioning, depending on roof type and approvals.
What is the payback period?+
Most residential systems pay back their cost in 4–6 years through electricity savings, after subsidy.
